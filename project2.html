Project 2  Sequencing Unsorted Data

We will collect, rearrange, and sequence digital images to create meanings that were previously unseen or not apparent. The project focuses on conveying a message through the accumulation and relationships of images, guiding the viewer to see the new connections from the reconstructed juxtapositions.
